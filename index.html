Mathmax Calculator

Mathmax is an accessible Windows calculator application specifically created for visually impaired and blind users. It provides an intuitive interface for both everyday arithmetic and complex mathematical computations, supporting both English and German languages.

Key Features

Standard Arithmetic & Constants

Perform basic calculations including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. The app fully supports grouping with parentheses and includes built-in access to mathematical constants like Pi and Euler's number (e). You can also easily calculate powers, standard and general roots, and factorials.

Functions & Equation Solving

Mathmax allows you to create, view, and remove your own custom functions. Furthermore, you can solve equations by specifying an interval (minimum and maximum values) to search for solutions.

Trigonometry & Calculus

Access standard trigonometric calculations (sine, cosine, tangent) and their inverse counterparts. The application also includes calculus tools, allowing you to compute the derivative of a function or find a derivative at a specific coordinate.

Probability & Statistics

Quickly analyze datasets to find minimums, maximums, averages, and standard deviations. For probability tasks, the calculator supports binomial coefficients alongside standard and cumulative binomial distributions.
